# Quarantine doctrine

Quarantine is not deletion and is not necessarily rejection.

A quarantined artifact may remain:

- historically important;
- technically useful;
- aesthetically valuable;
- a valid source of approved copy;
- a necessary reconstruction input.

It may not control production state until its release condition is met.
