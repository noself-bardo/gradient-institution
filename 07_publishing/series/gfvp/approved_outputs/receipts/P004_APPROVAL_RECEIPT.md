- exact metadata band

## Locked Format Doctrine

Boundary / Conversion Plates may use landscape orientation when the object requires a left-to-right relation between prior-state material, a central conversion boundary, and provisional output objects.

Landscape orientation for P004 is justified by conversion logic.

It is distinct from:

- P002 Gate / Boundary Plate landscape logic, which is justified by lateral threshold crossing
- P003 Ledger / Review Plate landscape logic, which is justified by ledger readability and disposition stamp spacing
