## Locked Format Doctrine

Ledger / Review Plates may use landscape orientation when the object requires row readability, disposition stamp spacing, and a formal review surface.

Landscape orientation for P003 is justified by ledger readability.

It is distinct from P002 Gate / Boundary Plate landscape logic, which is justified by lateral threshold crossing.
