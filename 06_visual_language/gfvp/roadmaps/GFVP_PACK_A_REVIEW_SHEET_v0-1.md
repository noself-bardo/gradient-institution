## Object Roles

### P011 - Decision

The smallest governance choice object.

It records selected path, rejected alternatives, rationale, consequences, review trigger, and status.

Primary risk: legal verdict, corporate decision tree, or software approval drift.

### P012 - Institutional Decision Record

The durable governance ledger for consequential decisions.

It records decision context, rationale, alternatives, evidence, consequences, status, owner, and review trigger.

Primary risk: legal filing, corporate policy memo, database screen, or compliance dashboard drift.

### P013 - Validation Gate

The test threshold where a candidate object is checked against practice, evidence, criteria, and steward review.

It can send material to Validated for Use, Revise and Retest, Hold for Evidence, Reject, or Promote Review Candidate.

Primary risk: software QA, grading sheet, certification badge, or final approval drift.

### P014 - Promotion Gate

The status-elevation threshold for validated material.

It can produce promoted material, remain validated, revise before promotion, hold for governance review, or route to constitutional review.

Primary risk: marketing promotion, career ladder, award badge, certification seal, or software release-pipeline drift.

### P015 - Canon Boundary

The constitutional boundary that prevents useful, admitted, validated, promoted, historical, or visually approved material from becoming canon by accident.

Only explicit constitutional ratification crosses this boundary.

Primary risk: mystical threshold, legal/royal authority, security access UI, government border, or automatic canonization.
