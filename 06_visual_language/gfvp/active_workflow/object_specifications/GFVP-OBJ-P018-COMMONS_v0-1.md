## Purpose

Commons provides a shared accessible knowledge field where participants can find and use material with visible source paths, status limits, and access boundaries.

## Constitutional Role

Commons matters because The Gradient needs common access without mistaking availability for archival custody, canon, product/platform behavior, public marketing, or social-media circulation.

## Boundaries

Commons is not Archive: Archive preserves custody, while Commons provides access. Commons is not Canon: common availability does not ratify authority. Commons is not Product or Platform: it is an institutional knowledge field, not a software surface. Commons is not public marketing and not social media; it does not optimize attention, broadcast identity, or turn knowledge into promotional content.
