## Purpose

Conflict / Contradiction holds incompatible claims, traces, or interpretations without forcing premature synthesis.

## Constitutional Role

It matters because unresolved tension must remain visible until review can route it responsibly.

## Boundaries

It is distinct from Uncertainty / Absence because conflicting material exists. It is distinct from Steward Review because it preserves the tension, while review evaluates what to do next.

## Inputs / Outputs

### Inputs

- claim A
- claim B
- evidence trace A
- evidence trace B
- review context

### Outputs

- held tension
- contradiction note
- review route
- use caution
- non-synthesis marker

### Non-Outputs

- resolution
- verdict
- blame
- canon
- automatic rejection

## Required Labels

- Conflict / Contradiction
- Claim A
- Claim B
- Held Tension
- Review Route
- Do not synthesize prematurely

## Forbidden Concepts

- canon by default
- visual approval as authority
- automatic promotion or ratification
- software interface or dashboard
- production workflow
- prompt engineering
- QC process as subject matter
- image gate as subject matter
- conversation summary
- mystical seal or certification badge
- object collapse into adjacent roadmap plates

## Diagram Family

Tension Plate.

Structure: Two opposed claim fields held in a central tension bracket, with review route visible but no resolution shown.

## Visual Center

Two opposed claim fields held in a central tension bracket, with review route visible but no resolution shown.

## Orientation

Landscape or near-square is acceptable when it improves legibility of the named structure.

No paper mockup, no shadows, no perspective, no UI screen, no dashboard, no app card, no corporate infographic, no colorful categories, no neon, no random glyphs, no prompt engineering, no QC document, no image-generation process, no image gate, no conversation summary, no automatic canon language, no final authority language, no invented metadata.
