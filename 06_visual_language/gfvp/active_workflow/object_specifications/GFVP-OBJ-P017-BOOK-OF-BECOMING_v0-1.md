## Purpose

Book of Becoming shapes selected traces into narrative memory that shows how The Gradient is changing, learning, and carrying unresolved transitions forward.

## Constitutional Role

It matters because The Gradient needs a living account of becoming that is neither constitutional doctrine, field-guide instruction, archive custody, commons access, nor promotional story.

## Boundaries

Book of Becoming is not Archive: it narrates selected traces rather than preserving all retained material. It is not the Constitution: it cannot ratify or define governing doctrine. It is not the Field Guide: it does not instruct practice. It is not Commons: it is a shaped narrative layer, not the general shared knowledge field. It is not promotional storytelling: it must preserve ambiguity, unfinishedness, and institutional learning rather than advertise success.
